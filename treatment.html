🇵🇭 Philippine Drinking Water Quality Standards

The Philippine National Standards for Drinking Water (PNSDW) are established by the Department of Health (DOH) to ensure safe and potable water for all Filipinos. These standards are based on World Health Organization (WHO) guidelines and are regularly updated to reflect current scientific knowledge.

All water service providers, including private systems like Siadeel Water, must comply with these standards to protect public health.

Source: DOH Administrative Order 2017-0010 — Philippine National Standards for Drinking Water (PNSDW) 2017
